Investor Relations

Investor relations (IR) is a section, present in record medium-to-large public firms that arrange for investors with an exact account of company activities.

This helps private and institutional investors make informed buy or sell decisions. A company's IR department also serves as a bridge for providing market intelligence to internal corporate management.

At Startup Mechanics our mission is to promote best practice in investor relations; to support the professional development of its members; to represent their views to regulatory bodies, the investment community and government; and to act as a forum for issuers and the investment community.
